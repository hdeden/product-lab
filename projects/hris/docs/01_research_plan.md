# 01_research_plan

## Purpose
- Validate HRMS scope, workflows, and priorities for cafe and security businesses in Indonesia.

## Inputs
- 00_exploration outputs.
- HRIS Design v0.1 research baseline.

## Outputs
- Research questions and hypotheses by user role.
- Research methods, participants, and timeline.
- Evidence to support or adjust MVP scope.

## Checklist
- [ ] Interview 3-5 cafe owners/managers about current HR workflows and pain points.
- [ ] Interview 3-5 security company managers about multi-location and 24/7 shift needs.
- [ ] Interview HR admins and finance on payroll, BPJS, and PPh21 workflows.
- [ ] Observe or map current processes: attendance, leave approval, payroll run.
- [ ] Test usability assumptions with operational staff (mobile-first, low-tech comfort).
- [ ] Validate pricing sensitivity (per employee per month ranges).
- [ ] Capture integration needs (POS, fingerprint device, bank transfer files).
