# Research Method

## Step-by-step
1) Define the question and scope.
2) List assumptions and unknowns.
3) Identify sources and prioritize them.
4) Gather evidence and capture notes.
5) Synthesize findings into insights.
6) Validate against requirements and constraints.
7) Summarize decisions and next steps.

## Operating rules
- Separate facts from interpretations.
- Track source quality and recency.
- Prefer primary sources when possible.
- Timebox deep dives and document tradeoffs.
