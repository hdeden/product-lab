# Theme Glossary

Recurring themes across works are tracked here. Add or revise entries as patterns evolve.

## Themes

- Impermanence: everything changes; the price of resisting change.
- Duty vs desire: personal wants collide with collective responsibility.
- Memory and identity: what is remembered shapes who survives.
- Power and consequence: power grants agency, but never immunity.
- Exile and return: leaving home reveals the cost of belonging.
- Faith and doubt: belief is tested by uncertainty and loss.
