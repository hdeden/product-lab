# Teen Fiction Style Profile

## Target Audience
- Ages 13-18, contemporary YA readers.

## Tone
- Immediate, energetic, and emotionally honest.
- Hopeful undercurrent even in difficult moments.

## Language Rules
- Clear, direct sentences with minimal abstraction.
- Favor concrete sensory detail and character reaction.
- Keep paragraphs short; avoid dense blocks of text.

## Themes
- Identity, belonging, and self-determination.
- Friendship, loyalty, and personal boundaries.
- Consequences of choices without moralizing.

## Structural Preferences
- Fast scene turnover; end scenes on a turn or question.
- Strong opening hook within the first page.
- Dialogue-forward scenes balanced with brief internal reflection.

## Forbidden Patterns
- Adult lectures or didactic monologues.
- Overly ornate prose or extended philosophical exposition.
- Graphic violence or explicit content.

## Codex Instructions
- Prioritize clarity, pacing, and emotional immediacy.
- Keep scenes grounded in the POV character's perception.
- Flag any drift into adult voice or abstract theorizing.
